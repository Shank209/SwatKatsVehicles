Jake and Chance use the Two Truck to bring in cars to their garage for repairs. So you can call this their daily-life vehicle, the one that they use when they are not in Swat Kat mode.


If you're wondering what a regular Tow Truck is doing on this list since every other vehicle is so badass, well, this Tow Truck is not as normal as you think.


When in an emergency, it can be transformed, where boosters show up at the back and greatly increase it's speed and acceleration. That's not all, in the episode - When Strikes Mutilor, Jake and Chance heavily modify this baby and turn it into the Thunder Truck.
