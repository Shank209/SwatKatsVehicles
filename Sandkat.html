The Sandkat is another light-weight vehicle like the Cyclotron and the Jet Ski, but built for travesring desert terrain. This vehicle is used is S1 E8 - Chaos In Crystal in which the Swat Kats need to operate in the Megakat Desert.


It looks similar to a snow-mobile and is like a desert version of it. T-Bone uses it in the episode to save Callie from Rex Shard and get back to the Turbokat, which is being repaied by Razor after crash-landing in the desert.


Although it doesn't seem to have any in-built weapons, it features some emergency thrusters that allow it to leap over large distances.
