The Hoverkat is a Swat Kat version of a hovercraft. It's a four seater with T-Bone and Razor riding in the two seats at the front. It's used in S2 E10 - Unlikey Alloys.


The Swat Kats use it to infiltrate Zed, a really giant robot, after teaming up with Molly and Dr.Greenbox although it gets taken out once they get inside Zed.


It's equipped with the standard Swat Kat weapon kit with missiles and stuff and has three thrusters at the back to propel it.
