First of all, the name of this vehicle is not actually Sub/Tank. It's an unnamed vehicle used in S1 E13 - katastrophe. It's like a hybrid of a submarine and a tank.


The Swat Kats use it to get the jump on Dark Kat when he captures Callie and the Mayor and imprisons them in an abandoned factory. This vehicle is first deployed in Submarine mode. In this mode its able to move underwater and it later transforms into Tank mode by bringing out three pairs of wheels and weapons now it can move on land and fire missiles.


The Swat Kats also use it to escape the blast safely after saving Callie and the Mayor when Dark Kat detonates a huge bomb, hoping to take out the Swat Kats and the others along with him.
