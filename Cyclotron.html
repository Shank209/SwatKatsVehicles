The Cyclotron is a badass mototcycle-like vehicle equipped with thrusters and weapons that allow the Swat Kats to get down to the ground level to reach areas that cannot be accessed in the Turbokat, like tunnels, etc. It first appears in S1 E2.


It's deploying process is one of the coolest things you get to see on the show. The Gunner, Razor, slides into the Cyclotron from the back seat of the Turbokat when it's in the air and the Cyclotron is shot out like a missile .


Once it touches down, its protective shell retracts, weapons point out and shit gets real. Usually , you see Razor driving the Cyclotron while T-Bone flies the Turbokat, allowing them to keep things under control both on land and in the air.


There is a two-seater version of the Cyclotron too that you don't see as often, which has both Razor and T-Bone riding it. It's used when they need it as the primary vehicle of a mission. The Cyclotron can also re-enter the Turbokat in mid-air once it's job on the ground is finished.
