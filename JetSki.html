The Jet Ski is a light-weight easily deployable vehicle for traversing over swamps. They are used in S2 E1 - Mutation City when Dr.Viper floods the city with slime, converting it into a huge swamp.


At one point in the episode, the Turbokat goes low on fuel and the Swat Kats land it on a building's rooftop and continue the mission on the Jet Skis. The deploying process of a Jet Ski is similar to that of the Cyclotron. It's shot out from the TurboKat like a missile, enclosed in a black protective shell.


The shell later comes off and falls away and the Jet Ski is ready to be driven. It's equipped with the Macrowave Blast, a pistol that emerges out from the Jet Ski which can be hand-held and used to shoot beams.
