The Thunder Truck is a heavily modified assault version of the Tow Truck that T-Bone and Razor normally use for their job. It's used is S2 E3 - When Strikes Mutilor, when the Turbokat wasn't ready to go airborne as it was undergoing some modifications.


This four-wheeler looks very similar to a Monster Truck with its huge wheels and sick paint-job. It has T-Bone driving it while Razor stands on the back, operating the Thunder Shock, a big gun mounted on the roof that shoots laser beams.


This baby also has two thrusters attached to the back that give the Thunder Truck additional boost needed to jump over long distances. The Swat Kats take out several of the Mutilor's marauders with the Thunder Truck although it is eventually destroyed by a big laser beam shot from Mutilor's mothership. T-Bone and Razor then go back to base using jetpacks, come back with the Turbokat and get their revenge.
