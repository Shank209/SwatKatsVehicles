The Turbomole is a vehicle specifically designed for missions that require the Swat Kats to operate underground. It's used it S2 E7 - Caverns of Horror, when they had to fight some mutated agracite scorpions in underground caves.


The most noticable thing about the Turbomole is the giant drill it's fitted with at the front. This allows it to dig straight into the ground and reach great depths very quickly. It's because of this that the Swat Kats were able to save Ann Gora who was captivated underground by the scorpion monsters.


It is deployed on ground from the Turbokat, with T-Bone in the front seat and Razor riding in the back, just like in the jet. It's equipped with the Mega Winch Missile which is a grappling hook shot from an elevated mechanism on the roof. It also has three Jet Boosters at the back that give the Turbomole extra thrust when needed to jump over gaps, etc.


At the end of the episode, the Swat Kats are jumped by a giant mutated scorpion and the Turbomole gets destroyed in the process. T-Bone and Razor immediately get their payback using the Turbokat though.
