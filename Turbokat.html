The Turbokat is the primary vehicle of the Swat Kats. It's a jet built from dumped aircraft parts at the salvage yard. This mean machine is capable of reaching speeds upto Mach 5 and with T-Bone as the Pilot and Razor as the Gunner, it's one of the deadliest thigs in the skies of Megakat City. It's first appearance is in S1 E1 and is the only vehicle which is used in every episode of the series.


It's equipped with some sweet hi-tech systems like Electronic Surge Detection, Dimensional Radar and X-ray scanning and also has communication links with the Enforcer network.


The Turbokat has two primary Mega Thrusters which propel the jet during normal flight. It also has a third mega thruster placed between the two primary ones which is used for vertical landing and take-offs and it also lets the Turbokat lift objects weighing upto 4 tons. The third thruster is modified later in the series, further increasing its performance.


If you think this is cool, just wait till you read what's next. The turbokat is later upgraded with four high powered rocket fuel power plants which let it enter Speed Of Heat mode in which its speed is temporarily boosted to insane levels of upto Mach 8.


It's also got one hell of a weapon arsenal including Octopus missiles, Scrambler missiles, etc apart from some awesome countermeasures. All this, combined with stealth, make the Turbokat the perfect recipe for badassery.
